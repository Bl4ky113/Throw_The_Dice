Rules To Play The Game

To Win The Game

When you give those dice a spin, the result must be greater than 6 in order to win; otherwise you can deposit your kidneys at the nearest redeem point.

Extra Score

You can win an extra score, you can redeem it at the store. But there's only lollipops and free bathroom passes. TP not incluided.

  • If your dice are a pair, you get the extra score. But if you still lose with your pair, you can say: "that's unfair".
  • You can get an extra score, if one of your dice is higher than 5. But if one of them is lower than 2, you lose extra score. Else if none of your dice enters in these categories, your score is average, boring if you ask me.

Be careful, you can also lose extra score, and that might cost you more.
